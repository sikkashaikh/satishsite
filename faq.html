Why use glass cleaner?

Glass cleaner removes dirt, grime, fingerprints, and smudges, leaving surfaces streak-free and clear. Itâ€™s easy to use and effective on glass, mirrors, chrome, and stainless steel.

Which glass cleaner is best?

Popular brands include Windex, Method, Seventh Generation, and Sprayway. Consider factors like streak-free finish, cleaning power, safety, scent, and eco-friendliness.

Can glass cleaner be used on a TV screen?

No, glass cleaner can damage the anti-reflective coating on TV screens. Use a soft, lint-free cloth instead, slightly dampened if needed.

What glass cleaner has ammonia?

Many traditional glass cleaners contain ammonia for cutting grease and grime. Always check the label and use in well-ventilated areas.

Can glass cleaner be used on mirrors?

Yes, it effectively removes smudges and fingerprints. Use a soft, lint-free cloth to avoid scratches.

Where to use glass cleaner?

Use on windows, mirrors, glass tabletops, picture frames, car windows, windshields, side mirrors, and headlights. Avoid using on TV screens.
